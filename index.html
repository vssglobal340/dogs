About Us

Project Stray Dog Liberation Universal is a compassionate initiative founded by VSS Global. We provide shelter, medication, food, and care for vulnerable senior dogs. Our goal is to ensure every stray dog has a safe haven, medical attention, and nutritious meals. We also promote spay and neuter programs to control the stray dog population humanely.

Our Care Programs

Shelter & Food

Our shelter provides a safe space for stray dogs, offering food and warmth to those in need.

Medication

We ensure that all dogs receive timely medical treatment and care for a healthy life.

Spay & Neuter

Our spay and neuter programs help control the stray population and improve overall health.
